About us

Philosophy of Art is a flourished realm. Its embodiment in its adepts is widely spreading. Accelerating its pace. Euphoric pace on it has been taken in more than a century now. Nowadays the new creative forms have expanded uniquely. An introduction needs to be set to clarify the field for a beginner. In what way and how should it be done?...

Philosophy of Art is a demanding ambit to present. Its subject of thought is puzzling in contrast with other zones of philosophy. In the theory of knowledge we don’t need to reflect beyond ourselves to attack the target. We may interpret philosophically our inner intellective activities of reflection, in other words, we all empirically perceive, think and assert to know things. Philosophers of Art need to tackle a wide realm which has blurred boundaries. Not only cope classic disciplines, but with not classified categories.

Crew

To delineate the nature of art is the core dispute, coping the Philosophy of Art.

We should discern among scientific, sociological, psychological approaches to Art, which reach draw their conclusions in an empiric manner. On the contrary aesthetics should commit to reality of Art; it shouldn’t be neither inductive generalization nor should be particular aesthetics judgements. On the other hand, philosophy of art aim to be Universal. It depicts endeavors to get the spirit. Being aware of the one-sidedness, we can enrich our hermeneutics if there is cross-fertilization among the philosopher, the artist, and the critic. Philosophical insight may be inherent in the critic´s aesthetical judgement; as well as the artist can reflect philosophically.
